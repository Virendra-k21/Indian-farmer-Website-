INDRODUCTION:

The Indian Farmer Website is developed to provide information about farmers and the journey of food . This website explains the Farm-to-Fork process, including the role of farmers, transportation of crops, market distribution and the importance of consumers. It helps to create awareness about problems faced by farmers such as low profit, middlemen involvement and food wastage. The main aim of this website is to promote direct selling of agricultural products and ensure fresh food for consumers at a fair price.
